Scoring System for H2H Points

The Head to Head Point system is as follows

Player Stat Category Value
Point Scored (PTS) 1
Missed Field Goal* -0.5
Missed Free Throw* -1
3-point Shots Made(3PTM) 1
Offensive Rebounds (OREB) 1.25
Defensive Rebounds (DREB) 1.75
Assists (AST) 1.75
Steals (ST) 2.25
Blocks (BLK) 2.25
Turnovers (TO) -1
Technical Fouls (TECH) -2
Double-Doubles (DD) 5
Triple-Double (TD) 20

*On Yahoo you are deducted 1 point for a field goal/free throw attempt and then awarded 1 point for a made field goal/free throw.
